How to mine ELF?

by Elf on Sat. 24th February 2018

Many folks are looking forward to the AELF mainnet (decentralized cloud computing blockchain network), allowing them to mine ELF on their personal computers and mining rigs. Until mining is enabled, ELF is currently distributed both via exchanges, as well as the Candy system. The candy system is a great way to earn additional ELF over the long timespan of 3 years.
