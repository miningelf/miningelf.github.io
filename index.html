How to mine ELF?

Are you among those who are looking forward to mining ELF on the mainnet? AELF, the decentralized cloud computing blockchain network, will allow anyone to mine ELF on their personal computers or mining rigs. Until mining is enabled, ELF can be earned for free via the Candy System. This program runs for three years. Have fun collecting your ELF!
